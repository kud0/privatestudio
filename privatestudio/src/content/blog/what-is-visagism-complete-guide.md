## What is visagism?

Visagism is a discipline that studies human face shapes to create visual harmony through hair, beard, and personal style. It's not about following trends — it's about finding what truly flatters you.

At **Private Studio Barcelona**, we apply visagism as the foundation of every service. Before cutting a single hair, we analyze the client's facial structure to design a style that enhances their best features.

## How does facial analysis work?

The visagism process is divided into three phases:

### 1. Face shape identification

There are seven main face shapes:

- **Oval**: Considered the most balanced. Most styles work well.
- **Round**: We aim to visually elongate with volume on top and shorter sides.
- **Square**: Angles are softened with textures and subtle fades.
- **Rectangular**: Lateral volume is added to balance the length.
- **Triangular**: A wide jaw is compensated with volume at the top.
- **Diamond**: We work with bangs and textures to soften the cheekbones.
- **Heart**: A wide forehead is balanced with styles that add weight below.

### 2. Proportion analysis

Beyond the general shape, we analyze:
- The proportion between forehead, nose, and chin
- Facial symmetry
- The hairline
- Hair texture and density
- The client's lifestyle and personality

### 3. Personalized design

With all this information, we create a unique design that:
- Enhances your best facial features
- Compensates for possible asymmetries
- Adapts to your daily routine
- Reflects your personality and lifestyle

## Why choose visagism over a standard cut?

The difference is clear: a standard cut follows a trend without considering whether it flatters you. Visagism **designs specifically for you**.

At Private Studio, we've seen clients who had the same cut for years discover a style that completely transforms their image. It's not just about looking good — it's about **projecting an authentic and well-groomed image**.
